Group:

To collaborate and to push and commit freely in GitHub repository, we used the method that invited the collaborator. Because we estimated that no need to grant any permission of one member is much more accessible. By inviting each team member to allow the access to the one certain repository, we could push and pull the outcome at each member’s local repository. Everyone could check the contributions of each member in the github repository recorded at Insights section. To say about the meeting, we had face to face meetings after the tutorial class every Wednesday. We formed our group in Week 5 and had 4 meetings from week 5 to week 9 after the class except week 7. We proceeded with the meeting for at least 20 minutes and in each meeting, we discussed and framed the plan. The regular meeting was also the chance to check if the plan progressed satisfactorily and if there is any problem or concerns.

  • 1st meeting (Week 5, 30 minutes) - Established the overall plan and proposed the framework. The IT Work part which required the contact with an IT professional and help of him/her was discussed as a high priority.
  • 2nd meeting (Week 6, 20 minutes) - Create the github repository (But a new repository was made at week 7 due to the continuous error we could not resolve.), Divided 4 of the area in the IT Technologies section who is going to take charge of which area.
  • 3rd meeting (Week 8, 25 minutes) - Developed the Project Idea which was determined in the virtual meeting. Organization and arrangement about the Interview that was conducted in the previous week.
  • 4th meeting (Week 9, 20 minutes) - Checked every part which should have been written. Discussed about Feedback and Group Reflection part.

    • Except that regular face to face meeting, the detailed things and concrete ideas such as team name, project idea, and further information about Industry Data were decided via virtual meeting in facebook group chat or discord in real time. We brainstormed each part that needed the whole member’s idea. Specific determination in Team Profile(compare and contrast the ideal jobs) and Tools section also decided through the online meeting during 5 weeks of time. About the HTML and CSS, every 4 members contribute to HTML and one member took charge of CSS. Each member pushes and commits their own part to the one common repository.
